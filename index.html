Instructions for students how to frame the condition

Weak Password

  • Length less than 8 characters.
  • Does not contain uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Medium Password

  • Length greater than or equal to 8 characters.
  • Contains at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number OR special character.

Strong Password

  • Length greater than or equal to 8 characters.
  • Contains at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character.
